# Meepo — North Star

## The Core Idea

Meepo is not primarily a tool.

It is a **mythology engine for tabletop storytelling**.

The software exists to capture, preserve, and celebrate the stories created by D&D tables across the world. These stories collectively form a living cosmic archive.

The product should always feel like **a world unfolding**, not merely a utility dashboard.

However, the mythology must always sit on top of **solid, invisible infrastructure**. The SaaS platform is the medium that enables the story.

> SaaS is not the product.  
> SaaS is the medium in which the myth unfolds.

## P0 Foundation Interpretation (March 2026)

This is an explicit guardrail for roadmap interpretation:

- The mythic sky/Archivist/community layer remains the product north star.
- P0 does not abandon mythology.
- P0 defers mythology as runtime/user-facing behavior while the SaaS substrate is made trustworthy.
- The intent is sequence, not priority inversion: reliable infrastructure first, mythic expression layered on top.

In short:

> P0 establishes the medium so the myth can be safely hosted.

---

# The Cosmology of the Product

The system operates across two surfaces with different narrative roles.

## Discord: The Adventure

Discord is where the game actually happens.

This environment must remain **as diegetic as possible**.

Interactions should feel like they belong inside a fantasy world rather than a software interface.

Early versions of the product may intentionally minimize bot speech to preserve immersion.

---

## Web App: The Observatory

The web application is where users step outside the story to observe the archive.

The web app is intentionally **non-diegetic**.

It provides:

- setup guidance
- operational controls
- session history
- campaign organization
- observability of the “sky”

It is the **astronomer’s observatory** for the stories told in Discord.

---

# The Sky Model

Stories are represented cosmically.

| Concept | Representation |
|------|------|
| Session | Star |
| Campaign | Constellation |
| Guild | Region of the sky |
| Global usage | The brightness of the heavens |

Each session contributes to a shared sky visible in the web application.

This creates a subtle sense of **community participation** across all tables using the system.

---

# The Alpha Narrative

During Closed Alpha:

- The Archivist (Meepo) has **not yet appeared**.
- The sky is simply **watching and listening**.
- Sessions quietly become stars.

Discord behavior should remain minimal and observational.

Possible subtle signals:

- a star brightening after a session
- a faint celestial acknowledgement

But the Archivist does not yet speak.

---

# The Beta Event

When the sky contains enough stars, the Archivist descends.

This is the moment when the character **Meepo** is revealed to users.

This event marks the transition from passive chronicle to active storytelling companion.

---

# Design Principle

Every feature should pass the following question:

> Does this strengthen the feeling that stories are being written across the sky?

If the answer is no, the feature should be reconsidered.

---

# Product Philosophy

Meepo exists to make players feel that their stories matter.

Every table is contributing something small to a much larger archive of adventures.

The ultimate goal is that a user feels this:

> "Our campaign is now part of the sky."

That emotional moment is the true product.
